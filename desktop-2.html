About Fresh to Order

We proudly prepare all of our proteins “fresh to order,” on our flame grill as you order them, then feature them in our signature panini, hot entrees, soups, salads and breakfast dishes. We'll prove to you that fresh food doesn't always take long to reach your door, and fast food can taste delicious every time.
